Manali Travel Itinerary

Plan your 5-day trip to Manali

Day 1 - Arrival at Manali

Check-in at the hotel, relax, eat local specialities and take a short evening walk nearby.

Day 2 - Sightseeing

Visit Hidimba Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, Manali Nature Park and such local sight-seeing places.

Day 3 - Extreme Sports

Enjoy extreme sports like paragliding,river rafting, skiing, snow activities, etc.. in Manali.

Day 4 - Rohtang Pass

Drive through the Rohtang Pass and enjoy the snow-covered landscapes and nature.

Day 5 - Departure

Spend a silent morning, shop for gifts, and leave for home with memories.
